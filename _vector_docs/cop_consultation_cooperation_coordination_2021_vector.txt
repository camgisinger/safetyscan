CHUNK 1: HSR List Display Requirement
Section: WHS Act s50–54; CoP s4.3
Topic: Mandatory display of health and safety representative names and work groups

MANDATORY DISPLAY:
- A list of names and work groups of all HSRs must be displayed in a prominent place at the workplace
- List must be visible to all workers

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- HSR list displayed on safety noticeboard in common area = compliant
- No HSR list visible on a large construction site = flag — workers may not have been informed of their rights
- HSR list outdated or illegible = non-compliant

CHUNK 2: Toolbox Talk and Consultation Records
Section: WHS Act s47, s48, s49; CoP s2, s3.1
Topic: Evidence of active worker consultation on hazards and risks

CONSULTATION EVIDENCE:
- Toolbox talk records showing date, topic, attendee signatures = documented consultation
- Risk assessments signed by both management and workers or HSRs = collaborative assessment
- Safety meeting minutes posted in common area = timely advice of outcomes

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- Workers participating in pre-start briefing or toolbox talk = active consultation occurring
- Toolbox talk sign-in sheets visible at site office = documented consultation process
- No evidence of any consultation process on construction site = non-compliant
- Workers unaware of site hazards or safety procedures = consultation failure
- New equipment introduced without evidence of worker consultation on risks = non-compliant
- Changes to work processes without toolbox talk or safety communication = consultation failure

---

CHUNK 3: Safety Noticeboards and Information Sharing
Section: WHS Act s48; CoP s3.1, s3.2
Topic: Physical display of WHS information and consultation outcomes

MANDATORY INFORMATION SHARING:
- Relevant WHS information must be shared with workers
- Workers must be advised of consultation outcomes in a timely manner

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- Safety noticeboards in common areas displaying current hazard and safety information = positive information sharing
- Written issue resolution procedure displayed in workplace = compliant procedural development
- Safety meeting minutes posted in common area = timely outcome communication
- Bilingual safety signage or translated materials = addressing diverse language needs
- Suggestion box or dedicated safety communication channel visible = multiple contribution channels
- No safety information displayed = information sharing failure

---

CHUNK 4: HSR Training Evidence
Section: WHS Act s50–54; CoP s4.3
Topic: Mandatory initial and refresher training for health and safety representatives

MANDATORY TRAINING:
- HSRs must complete initial 5-day training course
- HSRs must complete annual refresher training thereafter

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- HSR attending approved 5-day training course = compliance with training obligations
- Training certificates displayed or available for inspection = evidence of completed training
- HSR present and participating in management safety walkthroughs = inclusion in safety matters
- HSR without evidence of initial 5-day training = non-compliant

---

CHUNK 5: Health and Safety Committee Meeting Records
Section: WHS Act s75; CoP s4.3
Topic: Mandatory meeting frequency and composition requirements (KEY MEASUREMENTS)

MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS:
- Committee must meet at least once every THREE MONTHS (minimum 4 times per year)
- At least HALF of committee members must be workers NOT nominated by management

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- Committee terms of reference or constitution document visible = clear role definition
- Meeting minutes showing at least 50% worker (non-management) attendance = compliant composition
- Meeting schedule showing four or more sessions per year = compliance with quarterly requirement
- Meeting minutes dated more than 3 months apart = non-compliant frequency
- Management-only safety committee with no worker representatives = non-compliant composition

CHUNK 6: WHS Management Plan on Construction Sites
Section: WHS Regulation s309; CoP s5, s5.1
Topic: Mandatory plan for projects $250,000 or more showing consultation arrangements

MANDATORY FOR CONSTRUCTION:
- Principal contractor must ensure WHS management plan includes arrangements for consultation, cooperation and coordination between all PCBUs at site
- Applies to construction projects costing $250,000 or more

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- WHS management plan available on site listing consultation arrangements = compliant
- No WHS management plan on construction project costing $250,000 or more = non-compliant
- Shared site induction involving multiple contractors = positive coordination indicator
- Subcontractors on site unaware of site safety rules or emergency procedures = coordination failure
- Labour hire worker without site induction or safety briefing = shared duty coordination failure

CHUNK 7: Provisional Improvement Notices (PINs)
Section: WHS Act s50–54; CoP s4.3
Topic: HSR power to issue written notices for safety breaches

HSR POWERS:
- HSR may issue Provisional Improvement Notice (PIN) for a safety breach
- HSR may direct cessation of unsafe work in certain circumstances

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- PIN displayed at workplace or attached to non-compliant equipment = HSR exercising powers
- Work stopped by HSR direction with visible notice or communication = lawful HSR action
- PCBU refusing to acknowledge or respond to PIN = potential non-compliance

CHUNK 8: Union Representative Access
Section: WHS Act s45A, s45B; CoP s1.1
Topic: Worker right to union representation in safety consultation

LAWFUL REPRESENTATION:
- Worker may request registered union official be present at safety consultation
- Union must be one the worker is eligible to join and whose rules entitle it to represent the worker
- Must be registered employee organisation with Fair Work Commission or QIRC

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- Worker requesting union official be present at safety consultation = lawful exercise of representation right
- Union representative participating in site safety inspection or committee meeting = compliant representation
- PCBU refusing properly registered union representative access to safety consultation = potential non-compliance
